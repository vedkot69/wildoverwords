01 Discovery

Understand the brand before making content.

I start by studying your brand voice, digital presence, audience behavior, and category context so the strategy has substance from day one.

  • Content strengths, gaps, and missed opportunities
02 Strategy

Build a content system, not isolated posts.

I shape the narrative architecture: themes, hooks, formats, campaign directions, and the role each piece of content plays.

  • Platform-specific content pillars
  • Reel, caption, and storytelling direction
  • Monthly planning with clear intent
03 Creation

Translate strategy into content people remember.

Scripting, direction, visual storytelling, content writing, and production planning built around emotional and commercial pull.

  • Scripting and concept development
  • Shot direction and B-roll planning

04 Optimisation

Refine what works and compound the results.

I track retention, reach, saves, engagement, and resonance, then use those learnings to sharpen the next wave of content.

  • Performance review and learnings
  • Iteration on hooks, formats, and themes
  • Long-term growth through informed repetition
